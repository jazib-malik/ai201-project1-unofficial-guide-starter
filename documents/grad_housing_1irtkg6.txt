[1 points] maybe the situation for international students is different but i’ve never actually met anyone who lived in one of the on-campus grad apartments, with the exception of the UC village. off campus, people live all over, but north/central berkeley are some common spots. you can expect to pay around 1100-1500 for a nice single room in an apartment/house with roommates depending on how close you want to be to campus. biking is very common (as long as your office/building as a place to securely store it). as for bars, the ones closer to campus aren’t popular with grad students — most people i know go out in oakland or to breweries in west berkeley.
